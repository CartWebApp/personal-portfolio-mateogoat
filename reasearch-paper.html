Overview

In my English class, I wrote a research paper on how animal enrichment has been advancing. I reviewed high-quality sources to find how zoos have been tracking the day-to-day use cases of logging an enrichment.

Concepts Learned

  • Thorough Research: An important piece when creating a research paper is to find reliable sources and are legible enough to show your claims are true, which is exactly what I did for this essay. We used AI to assist us in finding sources but regardless it is important to do your research prior to publishing it out for the world to see.
  • Advanced Writing Techniques: Since I am in honors English class, it is required to know more than the bare minimum to get by. I learned how to use semicolons, dashes, and not just directly quoting from the text to make a more appealing argument to the reader.

Problem Solutions

  • Grammar Errors: In my original iteration for the essay, I had some syntax issues when I wrote about all of the enrichment information. However, after having peer editing rounds inside my English class, I revised the essay to make it a much easier read than previously.

Habits of Mind

  • Accepting Responsibility: Accepting the fact you may be wrong about something is hard for so many people, including myself at first. I realized that my writing is far from perfect, and I needed to perfect it heavily enough just to get by in such a difficult class.
  • Flexibility: As having lots of different work from every class I am enrolled in, I had to have flexibility when it comes to completing this essay on time and well. It may not have been perfect scheduling, but it paid off by submitting it without being marked as late.
