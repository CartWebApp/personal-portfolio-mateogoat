Overview

This is a webpage dedicated to Bio Shield’s anti-bug repellent, including a contact form, product listings, company services, an about page, and a purchasing process! This website was entirely coded with my knowledge of AI literacy.

Concepts Learned

  • AI Literacy: In the current workforce in web development, AI is being used as a tool to make work more efficient and quicker to deploy, and this project taught me how to use my AI literacy to make real-world applications.
  • Website deployment: At the beginning of my web development career, I was not sure how to deploy my applications to the world, and I learned that Netlify is a leader in front-end website publishing.

Problem Solutions

  • The Problem: I did not know where to begin on creating a website at the beginning of the development process.
  • The Solution: With my AI literacy, I researched what I need to implement into my website to make sure it fully functions as intended.

Habits of Mind

  • Communication: I spoke with my teammate heavily outside of school to find what was needed to be modified within the code. This is an important piece to know when coding with your peers because all developers must be on the same page in order to get work done.
  • Persistence: I was extremely dedicated in getting this website complete in such a short amount of time, even if my partner fully wasn’t. I spent countless hours finding a good prompt to have AI assist on making the website fully realized.
